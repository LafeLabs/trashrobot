MOTOR

TOOLS

  • SCREWDRIVERS, PHILLIPS AND FLAT HEAD: REMOVE ALL SCREWS THAT ARE ACCESSIBLE
  • PLIERS AND HAMMER:SMASH, BEND, PRY AS NEEDED

FEEDSTOCK

  • COMPUTERS
  • PRINTERS AND FAX MACHINES
  • SMALL MOTORIZED TOYS
  • ANYTHING SMALL THAT MAKES A WHIRRING NOISE

TARGET MOTORS

  • DC BRUSHED MOTORS: BIG MOTORS IN PRINTERS WITH TWO LEADS(12V OR 24V OR MORE) SMALL, WITH GEARS AND PULLEYS IN CD/DVD DRIVE DRAWER MOVER, SMALL MOTORZIED TOYS
  • STEPPER MOTORS: ALWAYS HAVE FOUR LEADS. BIG STEPPERS IN PRINTERS, SMALL ONES IN OPTICAL READ HEAD MOVER IN CD/DVD DRIVE

MOTORS TO AVOID

  • AC MOTORS: POWER TOOLS, KITCHEN APPLIANCES, LARGE MACHINES WITH NO CONVERSION FROM AC TO DC
  • DC BRUSHLESS MOTORS

TEST

  • MEASURE RESISTANCE
  • STEPPER MOTOR COIL IDENTIFICATION
  • TRY 5V, INCREASE UNTIL POWER IS SUFFICIENT

    • CONNECT WIRES AND INSTALL

    • SOLDERING, CONNECTORS, WIRES
