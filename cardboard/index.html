CARDBOARD FABRICATION

MATERIALS

  • CORRUGATED CARDBOARD
  • SCHOOL GLUE
  • BOX CUTTER
  • MARKER
  • RULER
  • NAILS
  • WEIGHTS

PROCESS

  1. FORRAGE FOR CARDBOARD
  2. MAKE AND CUT PATTERNS WITH RULER, MARKER AND BOX CUTTER
  3. APPLY GLUE AND STACK, HOLD DOWN WITH WEIGHTS WHILE GLUE DRIES
  4. ASSEMBLE WITH FRICTION, NAILS
