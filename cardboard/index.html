CARDBOARD FABRICATION

MATERIALS

  • CORRUGATED CARDBOARD

  • SCHOOL GLUE

  • BOX CUTTER

  • MARKER

  • NAILS

  • WEIGHTS

  • RULER
  • RUBBER BANDS
  • PAPER
  • PEN

PROCESS

  • FORRAGE FOR CARDBOARD

  • MAKE AND CUT PATTERNS WITH RULER, MARKER AND BOX CUTTER
  • APPLY GLUE AND STACK, HOLD DOWN WITH WEIGHTS WHILE GLUE DRIES
  • ASSEMBLE WITH FRICTION, NAILS
  • LABEL/DECORATE WITH PEN, PAPER, GLUE

TOOL HOLDER FOR XY PLOTTER
